About Valorant //

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter game developed and published by Riot Games. It combines elements of strategic team-based gameplay with precise gunplay, making it a thrilling and competitive experience. Players assume the roles of "agents," each with unique abilities, in a series of 5v5 matches where tactics and coordination are key to victory.

\\ Agents

In Valorant, players can choose from a diverse roster of agents, each with unique abilities and playstyles. These agents add a layer of strategy to the game, allowing teams to create synergies and adapt to different situations in matches. Mastering each agent's skills is key to achieving victory in the tactical gameplay.

Weapons //

Valorant offers a diverse arsenal of weapons, including rifles, submachine guns, shotguns, and pistols, each with distinct characteristics. Players can purchase weapons at the start of each round using in-game currency earned from previous rounds. Mastering weapon use and understanding their recoil patterns are essential for success in matches.
