Operator

"The ultimate long-range sniper rifle for one-shot headshots."

In-Game Stats

  • Fire Mode: Bolt Action
  • Damage:
    • Headshot: 202 (All Ranges)
    • Body: 255 (All Ranges)
    • Leg: 127 (All Ranges)
  • Magazine Capacity: 5
  • Wall Penetration: High
  • Cost: 4500 Credits

Real-World Inspiration

The Operator is inspired by the Accuracy International AX50, a bolt-action sniper rifle known for its long-range accuracy and high stopping power.

  • Manufacturer: Accuracy International
  • Caliber: .50 BMG
  • Magazine Capacity: 5
  • Effective Range: 1500m
  • Rate of Fire: Bolt Action
