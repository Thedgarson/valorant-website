    A vibrant map set in a coastal environment, emphasizing open spaces and verticality.

  • Bind

    A two-site map featuring teleporters that allow for quick rotations and strategic plays.

  • Lotus

    A unique map with three bomb sites, featuring rotating doors and a focus on close-quarters combat.

  • Pearl

    An underwater-themed map that offers a mix of long sightlines and tight corridors, encouraging diverse strategies.

  • Fracture

    A map with a unique layout that splits the teams at the start, allowing for unconventional strategies and quick engagements.

  • Breeze

    A large, open map set in a tropical environment, known for its long sightlines and wide spaces that favor snipers.

  • IceBox

    A vertical map set in a snowy landscape, featuring multiple levels and ziplines for dynamic movement and positioning..

  • Haven

    The only map with three bomb sites, promoting diverse strategies and requiring teams to split their defenses.

  • Split

    A map characterized by its verticality and tight chokepoints, encouraging strategic use of utility to control areas.

  • Ascent

    A balanced map set in a vibrant city, featuring a central open area that serves as a focal point for engagements.
